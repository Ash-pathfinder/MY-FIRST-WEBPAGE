Project Overview

TITAN is a custom-built flight computer designed to guide a 1km student launch vehicle and deploy a payload(Cansat) at the desired altitude . It integrates various sensors such as BMP280, ADXL345, GPS modules, and more. The system is powered by ESP32 Feather V2 running on RTOS, featuring real-time telemetry, Kalman filtering, and automated deployment controls.
